Welcome to the new CC50.

The CC50 has a new look, as well as being full of content and news!! CC50 is a completely free course!

This is Harvard's introductory Computer Science course, one of the best-known courses worldwide. Students learn a lot about how computers and how the Internet works, and about programming, so after finishing the course, you will have a great base to start your projects in Web Design, Databases, Electronic Systems, Software Programming, among others.

The course is challenging and demanding but it is completely possible to do, regardless of whether you already have experience in programming or not. Professor David J. Malan has incredible teaching skills and you will be able to learn even more! All class materials and Problem Sets were translated directly from the original material (CS50 2021 - CS50 license). This means you will learn everything that all Harvard students learn.

Additionally, you will learn to think algorithmically and see real-world problems from a new point of view. Here you learn things that can certainly be useful for students of any course, with any career ideal.

Another big new feature is the course development environment itself, better known as IDE. So, rest assured, you won't need to install anything to program! Note: if you choose to use the course IDE, you must have Internet access.

Another super new feature is our Discord server and our forum on Discourse. Too good, right?! At the end of the course, you will be able to generate your certificate from Fundação Estudar for the CC50 course.

The course was fully subtitled with you in mind, so take the opportunity to learn and even practice English!

CC50 is the Harvard course in Brazil!

O curso é desafiador e exigente mas é plenamente possível de ser feito, não importa se você já tem experiencia em programação ou não. O professor David J.
